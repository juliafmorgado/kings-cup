War Card Game

Computer

Result

Players

Just 1, the computer is the second player.

Goal

The goal is to be the first player to win all 52 cards

Rules

The computer suffles and randomly assign two cards at each round. The player with the higher card wins that round. If the cards are the same rank, it is War. Each player turns up one card face down and one card face up. The player with the higher cards takes both piles (six cards). If the turned-up cards are again the same rank, each player places another card face down and turns another card face up. The player with the higher card takes all 10 cards, and so on.

Card Values

J = 11, Q = 12, K = 13 and A = 14
